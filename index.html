About me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Aerospace Engineering and Mechanics at the University of Alabama, leading the Controls and Autonomy Lab (CAL). Before joining UA, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Mechanical Science and Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

My research interests include control theory, autonomous systems, aerial robotics, and applications to aerospace and agriculture. In particular, we develop and test control and decision-making algorithms to ensure the safe and efficient operation of autonomous systems, particularly in challenging and complex environments.
